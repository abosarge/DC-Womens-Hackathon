Red Carpet Careers

What Are Red Carpet Careers?

Crittenton Services of Greater Washington has created a program for girls to give guidance for career and education information. Career exposure improves youths' awareness of the career opportunities that lead to a family-sustaining living wage. Effective programs enable youth to learn about several careers and provide them with information about the skills and credentials required to enter and advance into the field of their choice.

The career profiles chronicle a “day in the life” of each career – career description, starting salary, education required, and what majors to pursue if interested in a particular career.
